Wargamers Pledge

The Pledge

I will paint or sell miniatures faster then I buy them.

To help us keep the pledge, we are building this website to track our buying habits and painting rates. By airing our progress in public we hope to shame ourselves into painting more figures.

These things are always easier when you are not alone though, so we are going to let anyone sign up and track their progress with us.
